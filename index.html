How to choose the best idea for your art

Description

The strategy is to give up the idea that you must finish every piece that you make. Instead, see each work as a trial or an experiment. Experiments mean that you're testing things, there isn't necessarily a definite yes or no, an experiment gives you more information to make new decisions.
